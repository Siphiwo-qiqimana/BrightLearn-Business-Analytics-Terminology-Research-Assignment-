This assignment is about learning the language of business analytics. Think of it like learning a new language for work. Every job has its own special words. In business analytics these words help people understand data. They help people ask the right questions about customers sales and money. They help people make smart choices instead of just guessing.

In this assignment you do not just memorize words from a dictionary. You research each word from real sources. You find at least five good places like textbooks or business reports. You read what experts say. Then you write the meaning in your own words.

The most important part is to show how similar words are different. For example Business Analytics and Business Intelligence sound like the same thing but they are not. Business Intelligence tells you what happened yesterday. Business Analytics tells you why it happened and what will happen tomorrow. You have to make that difference clear.

You also have to show how words connect to each other. For example you learn Customer Acquisition Cost which is how much money you spend to get one new customer. You also learn Customer Lifetime Value which is how much money that customer gives you over time. Then you learn the ratio between them. That ratio tells you if your business is healthy or dying.

When you finish this assignment you will speak the language that managers and analysts use every day. You will not just know words. You will understand what they mean in real life. That is the foundation for doing real analytics work.
